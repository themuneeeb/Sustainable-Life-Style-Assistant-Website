WASTE REDUCTION TIPS
  • Mindful Shopping:
    • Purchase only what you need and opt for products with minimal packaging or eco-friendly packaging materials.

  • BYO (Bring Your Own):
    • Carry reusable bags, water bottles, coffee mugs, and utensils to avoid single-use plastics and reduce waste.

  • Repair and Repurpose:
    • Extend the life of items by repairing them instead of replacing them. Get creative and repurpose items for new uses instead of discarding them.

  • Donate or Sell:
    • Give away or sell items you no longer need instead of throwing them away. This extends their usefulness and reduces landfill waste.

  • Compost Organic Waste:
    • Start a compost bin for food scraps and yard waste to create nutrient-rich soil.
